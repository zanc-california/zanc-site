ZANC Membership Benefits (Paid Members)
1.	Scholarship Raffle
o	Paid members can nominate eligible students from their families or communities—either in Northern California or Zambia—for a $500 scholarship raffle.
o	The winner will be drawn at the annual Independence Day celebration, promoting education and community growth.
2.	Discounted Event Entry
o	Enjoy discounted entry to ZANC’s major annual events, including:
	Independence Day Celebration
	4th of July Picnic
	Other cultural and community events.
3.	Free Participation in Community Activities
o	Participate in community-building activities and cultural events at no cost, fostering a stronger sense of connection and belonging.
4.	Support During Hardship
o	Funerals or Catastrophic Illness:
o	For paid members: 
	ZANC will take the lead at the family’s request, assisting with arrangements and community outreach.
	ZANC will contribute to a bereavement for nuclear family (Father, mother, children, siblings, spouse) an amount of $500
5.	Exclusive Membership Discounts (In Progress)
o	ZANC is working on securing exclusive discounts and benefits with local businesses and service providers to bring added value to your membership.
6.	Quarterly Community Mailers
o	ZANC will distribute quarterly mailers featuring:
	Community activities and updates
	Upcoming events
	A business showcase for any paid member who wishes to be featured, providing a platform to promote their services or initiatives.
These additions underscore ZANC’s commitment to fostering community engagement and supporting the endeavors of its members.

Broader Community Benefits (All Members)
1.	Cultural and Community Engagement
o	ZANC organizes cultural celebrations, educational programs, and community initiatives that foster a sense of unity and pride in Zambian heritage.
2.	Support During Hardship
o	Funerals or Catastrophic Illness:
	ZANC can provide support by disseminating details provided by the family to facilitate community assistance.
3.	Insurance Program Participation***
o	ZANC has organized and provides an opportunity for members to participate in a group insurance program through Hartford Insurance. Members are encouraged to take advantage of this benefit to ensure preparedness and peace of mind for unforeseen circumstances.***
4.	Networking and Visibility
o	Build relationships with other Zambians in the region, as well as connections with other communities and organizations.
o	Showcase your business, skills, or talents at ZANC events and through community platforms.
5.	Diaspora Representation
o	As part of the Zambian diaspora, you are a member of the “11th Province” of Zambia, with the power to advocate for and contribute to Zambia’s growth and development.
o	Engage in discussions and initiatives that connect the diaspora with Zambia, fostering progress both locally and abroad.
6.	Support for Zambia and the Diaspora
o	Through your engagement, ZANC strengthens its ability to provide visibility, support, and resources to Zambians in Northern California and contribute to initiatives back home.

The Importance of Community Membership
Being a ZANC member, whether paid or unpaid, enriches both your life and the community’s collective strength. Your involvement helps:
•	Grow the community’s visibility and influence.
•	Facilitate greater engagement with Zambia and other communities.
•	Foster opportunities for collaboration and support.
Together, we are building a vibrant, united Zambian diaspora in Northern California that honors our heritage, nurtures our present, and strengthens our future.
